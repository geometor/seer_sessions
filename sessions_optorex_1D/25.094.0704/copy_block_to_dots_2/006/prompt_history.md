3.  Create a new list called `output_list` as a direct copy of `input_list`.

    1. 3 <= i < len(sequence) - 1  (ensures i-1 and i+1 are valid for access/replacement)
    2. sequence[i] == non_zero_val
    3. sequence[i-1] != non_zero_val
